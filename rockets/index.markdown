## LV0

![LV0](/images/lv0.svg)

 **Length**               | 1.83 m
 **Empty Mass**           | 5.53 kg
 **Airframe Materials**   | Cardboard w/ 3 layers fiberglass
 **Recovery:**            |
  - Payload               | 1.2 m parachute
  - Body                  | 0.9 m parachute
 **Max Altitude**         | 0.3 km
 **Flights**              | 1: [L-0]()

No flight sequencing computer. Separation and recovery handled by chemically timed motor ejection charge.
